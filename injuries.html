1. Cuts & Scrapes

Healing Time: 3-7 days

Treatment: Clean with antiseptic, apply antibiotic ointment, and cover with a bandage.

Supplies Needed: Antiseptic wipes, antibiotic ointment, sterile bandages.

2. Bruises

Healing Time: 1-2 weeks

Treatment: Apply ice, elevate the area, and use pain relievers if needed.

Supplies Needed: Ice pack, compression bandage.

3. Burns (First-Degree)

Healing Time: 3-7 days

Treatment: Cool with running water, apply aloe vera, and cover with a non-stick dressing.

Supplies Needed: Cool water, aloe vera, sterile dressing.

4. Sprains & Strains

Healing Time: 1-3 weeks

Treatment: Follow RICE method (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation).

Supplies Needed: Ice pack, elastic bandage, pain relievers.

5. Blisters

Healing Time: 3-7 days

Treatment: Avoid popping, cover with a protective bandage, and keep clean.

Supplies Needed: Moleskin, blister bandages, antiseptic wipes.

6. Splinters

Healing Time: Immediate once removed

Treatment: Use tweezers to remove, clean with antiseptic, and cover if needed.

Supplies Needed: Tweezers, antiseptic wipes, antibiotic ointment.

7. Nosebleeds

Healing Time: Stops within 10-15 minutes

Treatment: Pinch nose, lean forward, and apply an ice pack.

Supplies Needed: Tissues, ice pack.
